# Cross-parliament framings of key migration targets


## TUR

### NLD (2903 mentions)

The NLD frames Turkey as a key partner in addressing migration through cooperation and agreements, emphasizing humanitarian concerns and the need for dialogue. Administrative and policy remedies focus on strategic planning and agreements rather than direct restrictions. Dominant frame: Solidarity and cooperation with Turkey.

**Dominant frame:** Risk and administrative framing with an emphasis on the EU-Turkey agreement and Turkey’s strategic position.

### BEL (1387 mentions)

Dominant frame: Administrative and policy-remedy framing.

### SWE (1022 mentions)

Dominant frame: Administrative and policy-remedy framing with concerns over migration agreements and financial contributions.

### CZE (690 mentions)
